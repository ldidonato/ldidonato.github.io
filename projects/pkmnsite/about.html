About

This website it dedicated to helping aspiring pokemon trainers complete the National Pokedex! For now this site only has information on #001 - #030 and locations only form Gen VI.

I made this website because of sites like Bulbapedia and Serebii had too much information to shift through to get what I wanted. All that is on this site is the pokemon in their National Dex order and where/how you can obtain them in Gen VI.

The search features are not live yet either.
