About Lauren

Graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Web and Mobile Computing at the Rochester Institute of Technology in Decemeber 2019.

At RIT she is an active member of Women in Computing and served as their webmaster for two years. WiC is an organization dedicated to promoting the success and advancement of women in their academic and professional careers, especially in technology. She frequently volunteers to teach young girls HTML and CSS at WiC events. She helped organize the all women's hackathon, WiCHacks, in 2019.

Has interned previously at EagleDream Technologies, Nationwide Mutual Insurance and Prudential Financial as a Web Application Developer.
